HumanLight: Celebrating Human Values

january 7 2026
7 min

HumanLight is a Humanist holiday celebrated annually since 2011, created to illuminate the shared values that unite humanity beyond religious or cultural boundaries. Rooted in secular humanism, HumanLight emphasizes reason, compassion, humanity, and hope—principles that guide ethical living and collective progress. This exhibition explores HumanLight not only as a holiday, but as a living philosophy expressed through art, ideas, and human connection.

The exhibition invites visitors to reflect on how reason empowers us to understand the world through critical thinking, science, and evidence, encouraging informed choices and thoughtful dialogue. Through visual works, interactive installations, and narrative displays, the theme of reason is presented as a tool for liberation—allowing individuals to question, learn, and grow while respecting diverse perspectives.

Compassion lies at the heart of HumanLight, and this exhibition highlights acts of empathy, solidarity, and care that define our shared humanity.
