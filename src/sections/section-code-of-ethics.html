Noӧdynamics Institute Code of Ethics

To be permeated by the quest of Academia and the culture of the West is central to life in the Noödynamics Institute. The Institute strives to represent the will-to-know of the culture-system without limitation, to allow ideas to develop freely, to remain skeptical yet open-minded, and to uphold truth unconditionally: Sapere aude!

Noödynamics exists only to the extent it is actualized through the Institute. How well this occurs determines the Institute’s quality. Because the theory of Noödynamics is metaphysical and the Institute’s practice is physical, the former cannot be perfectly realized in the latter. Translating thought into action inevitably reduces some of its existential vitality, requiring a constant tension between the transcendental idea and the Institute as a corporeal entity.

While institutional structure is indispensable for advancing a grand idea, institutions can perceive themselves as ends rather than means. Only constant vigilance ensures the Institute functions in service of its ideal. Members must demonstrate the highest intellectual and professional integrity. To remain dynamic, the Institute engages even with those who resist its aims, creating opportunities for fruitful dialogue and debate, ensuring scholarly quality and achievement guide its work.

Institute Member Statement of Academic Values

This statement presents the core values of the Noӧdynamics Institute. These values provide the foundation for the Code of Ethics of members and set the academic standard for scholars. These values are the fundamental beliefs that guide out proclamations and practices.  We believe that our values are vital to the integrity of Academia as a whole.
