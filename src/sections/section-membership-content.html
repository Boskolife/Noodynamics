Why is this intellectual individualization necessary? Because our civilization needs information, not merely figures, ideas, or presentations. While any one person may fulfill two or even all three roles, society and our mission require a degree of specialization due to the nature of our Academia. Certain disciplines of the Noödynamics Institute demand sustained intellectual focus from raw data to polished presentation. The Institute must also preserve integrity and professionalism between its internal processes and its engagement with the outside world.

The Noödynamics Institute intends to draw great power from the recovery of the University as an institution of High Culture. The Institute’s emergence as a key cultural force across the West, together with the fact that the University’s paradigm derives from Culture rather than the reverse, will converge in a demographic contribution to the Second Religiosity. Members of the Noödynamics Institute are unapologetically elitist and contemptuous of bourgeois mediocrity—the academic status quo embodies Nietzsche’s Last Man, while we represent a New Dawn. We pursue liberty and equality as means of releasing the best in all, allowing the elite to ascend naturally rather than suppressing this process. The Institute is an intellectual revolt within the establishment and therefore not bound by its limitations. Members are not required to do anything; they are only expected to be. Even passive participation serves a purpose, as the Institute evokes autochthonic energies that move through thresholds and phase transitions. Wherever two or more are gathered in its name, there our Culture-soul dwells.
